# Section & Component Taxonomy

This document defines the modular building blocks for the Alderman Law Firm website. Each component is designed to reinforce **Authority, Clarity, and Trust** while avoiding marketing fluff.

## 1. HERO (`HeroSection`)
*   **Purpose:** The Opening Statement. Establish the premise immediately.
*   **Psychology:** Primacy Effect. A clear first screen implies a clear legal mind.
*   **Structure:**
    *   **Headline (H1):** The Verdict. High contrast.
    *   **Subhead (Body Lg):** The Evidence.
    *   **Visual:** Abstract texture (stone/paper) or architecture. No "shaking hands."
    *   **Interaction:** Static. No carousels.

## 2. AUTHORITY / CREDIBILITY (`AuthorityGrid`)
*   **Purpose:** Define philosophy and core tenets ("Appellate Focus", "Flat Fees").
*   **Psychology:** Competence. Demonstrates a worldview, not just a service list.
*   **Structure:** 2-3 column grid. High whitespace. Icon-supported (outline style).

## 3. PRACTICE AREA INTRO (`PracticeGrid`)
*   **Purpose:** Navigation. Direct the user to their specific problem.
*   **Psychology:** Categorization. Reducing anxiety by helping the user find "their box."
*   **Structure:**
    *   Grid of Cards.
    *   **Card:** Eyebrow (H4), Title (H3), 1-sentence summary, text link.

## 4. ATTORNEY PROFILE (`AttorneyBio`)
*   **Purpose:** Humanize the authority.
*   **Psychology:** Trust Transfer. Clients hire humans.
*   **Structure:**
    *   **Image:** B&W/Desaturated Portrait. Direct eye contact.
    *   **Bio:** Narrative flow.
    *   **Sidebar:** Credentials (Admissions, Education, Clerkships).

## 5. CONTENT / ARTICLE (`ContentArticle`)
*   **Purpose:** Demonstrate SME status through writing.
*   **Psychology:** Authority by Association. "They wrote the book on it."
*   **Structure:**
    *   Prose container (65ch max).
    *   Meta data (Date, Topic).
    *   Academic footnotes.

## 6. FAQ (`FaqAccordion`)
*   **Purpose:** Pre-empt objections.
*   **Psychology:** Cognitive Ease. Breaking complexity into toggles.
*   **Structure:** Accordion (one open at a time).

## 7. CONTACT / INTAKE (`ContactSplit`)
*   **Purpose:** The Closing Argument / Call to Action.
*   **Psychology:** Conversion. "Review Your Case" (Benefit) vs "Submit" (Cost).
*   **Structure:** Split layout. Contact details left, Secure Form right.

## 8. TRUST SIGNALS (`TrustBar`)
*   **Purpose:** Third-party validation.
*   **Psychology:** Social Proof.
*   **Structure:** Bar of monochrome logos (Courts, Bar Associations).
